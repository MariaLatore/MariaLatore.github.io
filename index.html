Topics of Research

My research area in mathematics is analysis with interest in mathematical physics and applied mathematics. Specifically, I am interested in

  • Random matrix theory and determinantal point processes
  • Riemann-Hilbert problems and asymptotic analysis
  • Multiple) orthogonal polynomials and special functions, including Painlevé equations
  • Approximation theory
